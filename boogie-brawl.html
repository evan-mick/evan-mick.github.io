Boogie Brawl

The rhythm based fighting game

Boogie Brawl is a rhythm based dancing fighting game. I was a designer and programmer for the project.

Using Unity, C#, and ProBuilder, I programmed the player controller as well as easily usable level mechanics such as boost pads, enemy spawners, and designed the game's second level (one part pictured above).
